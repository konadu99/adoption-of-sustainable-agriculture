Local Resources (Kentucky)

Educational and Extension Services

  • University of Kentucky Cooperative Extension Service - Provides soil health workshops, farm field days, and demonstration plots
  • University of Kentucky IPM Program - Offers scouting services, pheromone traps, and training in pest identification
  • University of Kentucky Water Quality Extension Initiative - Promotes soil testing and nutrient management plans

Government Programs and Agencies

  • USDA NRCS - Kentucky - Administers EQIP and CSP programs providing financial assistance and technical support
  • Environmental Quality Incentives Program (EQIP) - Provides financial assistance for sustainable practice adoption
  • Conservation Stewardship Program (CSP) - Offers financial incentives for implementing sustainable practices

Local Organizations and Networks

  • Kentucky Soil Health Coalition - Promotes soil health and conservation practices
  • Kentucky Beef Network - Provides research and support for rotational grazing and pasture improvement
  • Kentucky Soil Health Partnership - Collaborative network supporting no-till systems and cover cropping
  • Local farmer networks and conservation districts - Community-led initiatives for sharing experiences and collective learning
